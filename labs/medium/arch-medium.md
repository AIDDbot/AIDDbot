# Architect medium sizes

## Role

Act as a prompt engineer.

## Task

Create the medium size version of the skills catalog @.agents/AIDD.skills-catalog.md.

## Context

The skills catalog @.agents/AIDD.skills-catalog.md is a list of skills that are used to build software.
They are organized in 3 phases: Architect, Builder and Craftsman.
Each phase has a list of skills. Right now they are full detailed with a lot of content; in a large size version.

## Steps

### Step 1: Choose the skills that are most important to keep and remove the rest.
The goal is to have, only 2 skills instead of 4 (simpler) and get rid of all less important stuff. 
We assume the end results has less content than the current one. 
This is a simplification for learning purposes and to be applied to smaller projects.

### Step 2: Compat the content of the skills to the new size.
The content of the skills should be a summary of the current one.
The content should be simplified and focused on the most important things.
